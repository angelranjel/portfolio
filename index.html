Hello! Welcome To My Travel Blog

Oregon

This gallery contains a few of the best photographs from my Summer 2017 trip to the state of Oregon's Pacific coastline. Also, included in this gallery are photographs from other fascinating places found within "The Beaver State", like Crater Lake, Columbia River Gorge, and Spirit Falls. The gallery will continue to be updated with more photographs soon.

Great Northwest

During the Summer 2019 my brothers and I adventured out on an 18-day trip in the Greater Pacific Northwest region. This gallery captures some of my favorite photographs that highlight some of the best parts of our long journey. View photographs from five different national parks including Yellowstone, Glacier, and Mount Rainier. The gallery will continue to be updated with more photographs soon.

Seattle

At the end of our wonderful 18-day trip of the Greater Pacific Northwest we decided to spend a few days wandering the beautiful port city of Seattle, Washington. In this gallery is a collection of handpicked photographs that attempt to capture the best sights, sounds, and smells of the “Emerald City’. The gallery will continue to be updated with more photographs soon.
