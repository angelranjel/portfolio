Adrift in Emerald City

After our exhausting trip across the Great Northwest of the country, my brothers and I decided to spend a few days rest exploring the beautiful port city of Seattle, Washington. As part of tradition, we try to catch a game of major league baseball at whatever major city we are visiting. So accordingly, we bought some seats at T-Mobile Park to watch the Seattle Mariners take on the Tampa Bay Rays under the big lights. We also got a chance to attend a major league soccer game when Seattle’s beloved Sounders took on the New England Revolution at Century Link Field downtown Seattle in an electric match that ended in a 3-3 draw. We got to tour Seattle’s famous Public Market and got to taste and smell foods from all corners of the world. To end our last day of our long journey, we rented a sailboat for a sunset cruise to get a different view of Seattle from the water. We got to see seals and dolphins splashing around as we made our way back into the harbor.
